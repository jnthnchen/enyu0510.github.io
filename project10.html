Stormwater Management Consulting

This capstone team project focused on designing a website for the University of Maryland Campus Community Connections program that includes information resources and arguments for the funding of stormwater management across all levels of government. Our final deliverables included a website design proposal as well as a dashboard.

The course of this project followed project progression steps that are reflective of what occurs in the real world. These included team contract, background research, context analysis, requirements interview, and project plan. I mainly worked on the website designs and also served as the point of contact between the client and my team.
