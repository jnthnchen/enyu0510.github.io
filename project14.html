Salaries Data Science

For this project, I used a recent salary information data set from Kaggle. The salaries are of data-related positions and the data includes fields such as job title, experience level, and remote ratio (0-100 where 100 is fully remote). To start, I cleaned the data using OpenRefine and grouped similar job titles together, such as grouping ML Engineer with Machine Learning Engineer. Next, I did some basic data analysis and visualization using Python to answer some questions I had about relationships between the fields. Then I used the Elastic Net and Lasso regression models tested by 10-fold cross validation to see how well they predict salary based on job title and experience level (excluding the executive position as there were only a few).

What I found interesting is the high remote ratio among all experience levels, which makes sense due to the pandemic and general shift towards remote work. The models used job title and experience level to predict salary. In terms of the closeness between predicted and actual values, Elastic Net predicted at ~58% accuracy and Lasso predicted at ~63% accuracy.
