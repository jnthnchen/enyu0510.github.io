Website Modernization for AusculTech Dx

AusculTech Dx is a Children's National startup that specializes in pediatric heart murmur care. I was given the opportunity to redesign their existing website with the goal of modernizing the look. I worked in HTML/CSS/JS and implemented new features into the design such as slideshow, typerwriter animation, and dynamic map.
